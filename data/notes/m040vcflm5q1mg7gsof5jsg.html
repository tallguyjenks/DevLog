Math Environment

to use LaTeX math you need to create a math environment:

$MATH GOES HERE$

When rendered this will look like: MATHGOESHEREMATH GOES HEREMATHGOESHERE

This is an in-line equation

For a multi-line equation that also centers itself you use double dollars

$$MATH GOES HERE$$

Rendered it will center: MATHGOESHEREMATH GOES HEREMATHGOESHERE

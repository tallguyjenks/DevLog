

## Data List

```html
<input list="animals" name="animal" id="animal">
<datalist id="animals">
    <option value="Cat">
    <option value="Dog">
    <option value="Chicken">
    <option value="Cow">
    <option value="Pig">
</datalist>

<!--
The <datalist> id attribute (see bold items above) 
must be equal to the list attribute of the <input>, 
this is what binds them together.
-->

```

#### [[T-SQL INSERT INTO]]

---

```sql
INSERT INTO database.schema.table (<col1>, <col2>, <col3>)
		VALUES (<val1>, <val2>, <val3>)
-- OR
INSERT INTO database.schema.table
SELECT <columns>
FROM <other_table>
```
